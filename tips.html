Learn More About Birds

Hi! Welcome to the Bird Lovers Club! Here you will embark in a humourous and laid back journey to fall in love with those wonderful creatures. I would like to share with you some my bird adventures through my drawings and stories of my encounters with some of the birds I met in Ireland. This club is for everyone! All you need to do is to connect with the birds to join it and if you feel like getting together, you can join me too in my birdwatching and art making adventures.
