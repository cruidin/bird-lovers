Blackbird

Blurb about blackbirds goes here. What can we say about blackbirds? Well, the male is black but the female is brown but the female is not called brownbird. Blackbirds like feeding on the ground. They have a beautiful bright orange bill.

Blackcap

Blurb about blackcaps goes here. I was doing some work last winter when I spotted a bird I hadn't seen in my garden before. It had a black cap. Yes, that also happened to be his name: Mr Blackcap. From that day on he came back everyday. He was particularly fond of the fat balls. Then one day another bird that I hadn't seen in my garden before appeared. Well, this one had a brownish red cap. It turned out she was Mr Blackcap's wife. And, just like the female she goes by her husband's name, Mrs Blackcap, and NOT Brownishredcap.
