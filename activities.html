Would you like to flock together?

We meet up every month to make bird inspired art, go bird watching and meet up for coffee.

Birds have been also an endless source of wonder (and dare I say envy) for humans since we existed. It’s also possible that other creatures that came before us were also a bit envious of birds and their beauty and their free nature. But that’s just speculation.
