Things You Can Do That Are Fun


There are endless activities that you can do using birds as an inspiration. Some of them are pretty obvious like birdwatching or gardening to bring more birds and wildlife to your garden and they're all excellent things to do.


I am listing just a few to get you started and inspired but you can really get creative here and maybe do something like dancing inspired by birds movements, or you could do some meditation inspired by the otherworldly nature of birds, or you could go for a run and pretend to be an ostrich.


On a more mondane level if you're on a plane you could imagine that the plane is a massive bird that is taking you to a magical land, or if you're bored you can just scream like a magpie, or if someone is rude to you you can surprise them by suddenly acting like a starling (starlings are wonderfully crazy and rude - I love them) and you've got their attention you start asking them over and over again "are you ready for the murmuration?" just to confuse them and piss them off.


Really, the options are plentiful.

Would you like to flock together?


We meet up every month to make bird inspired art, go bird watching and meet up for coffee. You can read more about what we do below and if you dicide to give it a go you can fill out the form and we will email you with more details about our upcoming meetings. All sessions are free! Well, except for the coffee.


Drawing


Where?

We meet up in different outdoors venues and we invite our members to come up with suggestions of where we will meet next.

When?

Our drawing sessions happen once a month on the first Saturday of each month at 10 am

What we do?

We sit down and... draw... guess what? birds!

How long?

A drawing session lasts about 1 hour.

What you need to bring

A sketchbook and a pencil are advisable but feel free to bring whichever medium you like to work and experiment with.

Sign up here!
